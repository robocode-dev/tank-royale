# Goals

G-xxx: who wants the system and why — the reason anything else in this corpus exists.

A goal artifact answers three questions: who wants it, what they want, and why it matters. New wishes enter as `status: proposed` — the proposed goals **are** the inbox; a human promotes a goal to `accepted` when the project commits to it, and `retired` closes it. Plans link the goals they serve, so every milestone traces back to someone who wanted it.
